It's Never too Late to Promote Heat Safety

First on the Market Portable Heat Safety Product

About Us:

Too Hot to Handle, is a small tab which is placed on or near any object or surface that reaches a burning level of heat. This tab contains thermochromic technology which changes color to alert you when a nearby object is “too hot to handle”. This tab also contains a bluetooth device that is connected to a separate motion sensor device that will beep if another object(hand) is coming near to something that is “too hot to handle”. It combines the capability of both heat detectors and the portability of temperature indicating stickers.
